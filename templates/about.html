So… who are you?

I’m Mike Tigas and I’m a freelance Web developer. Most of my work is in Python/Django programming. My expertise is in Web applications, though I also have experience with iPhone application development and GIS/mapping tools.

Clients and brands I’ve worked with include: The Spokesman-Review (Spokane, Wash.), KOMU-8 TV (NBC affiliate; Columbia, Mo.), and Infinity Ward’s Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. For more details on projects I’ve worked on, visit my portfolio.

Elsewhere: I help maintain GeoPy, an open-source geocoding toolkit for Python. I also occasionally open-source snippets, tools, and projects on Github.
